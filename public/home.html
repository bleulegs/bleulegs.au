About Us

Team Bleu Legs started as a after school Robotics group located at Calamvale Community College, Overtime as our knowledge grew so did our team, in only 2 weeks we went from a 4 person team to a 8 person team. Our first Competition was a FLL or First Lego League Regional Comp in Brisbane, It was tough but we pushed through, In the end as a team we achieved a Robot point score of 160 and a 4.5 / 5 in our innovation project, making us top 5 in the Competition meaning we would be advancing to Nationals. After long days and late nights it was finally time for Nationals, This time in Gladstone with 48 teams competing we got 165 points and a 5/5 Innovation Score, This only put us in 23rd place due to our robot Game, But out of no Where our names got announced to congratulate us on making it to APOC or Asia Pacific Open international Championships, A massive achievement, so for the next 8 months our team will be working to the best of our abilities to hopefully win the international championships putting us in the top 0.001% of the WORLD!
