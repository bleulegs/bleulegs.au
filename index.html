FIRST LEGO League National Award Winners

Who we are

We are Team Bleulegs, a team consisting of Year 9 & 10 national award-winning students from Calamvale Community College in Brisbane, Australia. Our team thrives on innovation, collaboration, and a shared passion for technology and engineering. We are committed to pushing the boundaries of robotics and STEM education, and through our dedication and teamwork, we have earned recognition at the FIRST LEGO League competition.
